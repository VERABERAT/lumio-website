Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Lumio, your privacy is not an afterthought — it is foundational to everything we build. This Privacy Policy describes how Lumio ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the Lumio mobile application and associated services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this policy carefully. By using Lumio you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide a great journaling experience.

  • Account information: When you create an account we collect your email address and a password hash. We never store your plain-text password.
  • Journal entries: Your entries are encrypted on your device before being transmitted to our servers. We use AES-256 end-to-end encryption so that your journal content is never readable by Lumio staff.
  • Mood and emotion data: Optional mood tags and emotional check-ins you log are stored encrypted alongside your entries.
  • Usage analytics: We collect anonymised, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., feature usage frequency) to improve the app. This data cannot be linked back to you.
  • Device information: Basic device details (OS version, app version, device type) are collected for crash reporting and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Lumio Service.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Sync your encrypted journal data across your devices.
  • Respond to support requests and bug reports.
  • Send you important service updates (you may opt out of marketing emails at any time).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising. Full stop.

3. AI Processing & On-Device Intelligence

Lumio's AI insights and emotional analysis features are designed with a privacy-first architecture. All natural-language processing used to generate insights runs on your device using on-device machine learning models. Your raw journal text is never sent to an external AI API or third-party model provider.

Aggregated, anonymised signals (not your journal text) may be used to improve our on-device models. You can opt out of this in Settings › Privacy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your encrypted data is stored on servers located in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany). We use industry-standard security practices including TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest, and regular third-party security audits.

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you by email within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data — including encrypted journal entries — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including the right to access, correct, port, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lumio.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Lumio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use of Lumio after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@lumio.app.
