Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lumio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Lumio mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Data: Email address and authentication tokens via Apple Sign-in or Firebase Auth.
  • Journal Entries & Mood Data: The text you input and the mood you select.
  • Subscription Data: Handled securely via RevenueCat and Apple. We do not store your credit card information.

3. How We Use Artificial Intelligence

Lumio uses Firebase Vertex AI to analyze your journal entries and generate personal insights, detect cognitive distortions, and provide emotional reframes. Your text is processed securely by our AI provider solely for the purpose of generating these features within the app. We do not sell your personal data or use your private journal entries to train public AI models.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Cloud's Firestore. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have full control over your data. You can request access to your data or delete your account entirely at any time directly within the Lumio app via the Settings menu. Deleting your account will immediately erase all your journal entries, AI insights, and behavioral reports from our databases.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@lumioapp.com
