Privacy Policy

Last Updated and Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lumio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This comprehensive Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Lumio collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Lumio mobile application. By using Lumio, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our core services, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Email addresses and unique user identifiers obtained through Firebase Authentication or Apple Sign-In. We do not require your real name.
  • User-Generated Content: The text of your journal entries, selected moods, and related emotional metadata.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as session duration, feature usage (e.g., AI Insights accessed), and crash reports to improve app stability.
  • Financial Information: We do not collect or store your credit card details. All subscription transactions are securely processed by Apple App Store and tracked via RevenueCat using anonymized app-user IDs.

3. How We Process Data with Artificial Intelligence

Lumio utilizes Google's Firebase Vertex AI to analyze your journal entries and generate insights, cognitive distortion detections, and reframing exercises. Please note:

  • Your text inputs are transmitted securely to our AI provider strictly for generating real-time insights within your app session.
  • No Public Training: We do not use your personal journal entries to train public foundational AI models.
  • The AI processing is automated, and no human reads your private journal entries.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We share necessary information with trusted third-party services exclusively to operate our application:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore: For secure cloud database storage and user authentication.
  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions and entitlement verification.
  • Apple: For processing payments and Apple Sign-In authentication.

These third parties are bound by strict data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your personal data for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. You have the absolute right to delete your data. By using the "Delete Account" feature within the Lumio app settings, the following actions are triggered immediately:

  • All your journal entries, AI-generated insights, and monthly reports are permanently erased from our active Firestore database.
  • Your user authentication record is deleted.

Note: Uninstalling the app from your device does not automatically delete your data or cancel your active subscriptions.

6. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Global Standards)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • The right to erasure ("Right to be forgotten"): You can execute this directly via the in-app deletion tool.
  • The right to restrict processing: You can opt-out of certain data collections, though this may limit app functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Lumio is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain European jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: support@lumioapp.com
